## CSS Flexbox

## What it is

Flexbox controls layout along one axis at a time. It is ideal for nav bars, galleries, button rows, and card arrangements.

## Why it matters

- Makes alignment easier than older float-based layouts.
- Supports wrapping and spacing for responsive UI.
- Gives agents a clear concept-to-project mapping for layout skills.

## Common errors

- Applying `justify-content` to items instead of the flex container.
- Forgetting `flex-wrap` in gallery layouts.
- Mixing up main axis and cross axis.

## Verification steps

1. Resize the viewport and confirm items wrap as intended.
2. Change `justify-content` and verify horizontal distribution.
3. Change `align-items` and verify vertical alignment.
